# Jaavo

**A zen garden for your job search.**

Jaavo turns the anxiety of job hunting into something calmer. Every application you add is a seed planted in your garden. As you move jobs from *saved* to *applied* to *interviewing* to *offer*, the seed grows — into a sprout, then a flower, then a tree. Rejected applications don't disappear; they become weathered stones resting in the garden, a quiet reminder of the work you've done.

---

## What Jaavo does

- **A simple kanban board** for every application, from saved to offer
- **Auto-fill from job postings** on LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and Handshake — just share the link
- **Gentle goal tracking** — set your own pace, daily, weekly, or by deadline
- **Interview reminders and follow-ups** that don't feel like notifications nagging you
- **A living garden** that reflects every application you've added
- **No accounts. No servers. No ads.** Everything stays on your device.

---

## Why a garden?

Job hunting is long, quiet, and often discouraging. Most trackers treat it like a spreadsheet. Jaavo treats it like tending something that grows — a small, calm ritual you return to each day. Progress is visible. Rejection is a stone, not a failure. The garden reminds you that consistency is the point.

---

## Private by design

Jaavo has no accounts and no server. Nothing you enter — companies, job titles, notes, goals — ever leaves your device. If you delete the app, your data is gone. That's the tradeoff for a tool that respects your privacy completely.
