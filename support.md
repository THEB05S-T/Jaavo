## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my data stored on Apple's servers or yours?
Neither. Jaavo has no accounts and no server. Everything you enter — companies, job titles, notes, goals — is stored **entirely on your device**.

### What happens to my data if I delete the app?
If you delete Jaavo from your device, **all of your applications and notes will be permanently lost**. There is no cloud backup because the app is fully local. If you want to preserve your data, do not delete the app.

### Does Jaavo back up to iCloud?
Jaavo's data is included in your device's standard iCloud or iTunes backup (if you have backups enabled in iOS Settings). Restoring your device from a backup will restore your Jaavo data. Jaavo itself does not sync between devices.

### How do I cancel my Jaavo Pro subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not by Jaavo. To cancel:
1. Open the **Settings** app on your iPhone
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Tap **Jaavo** → **Cancel Subscription**

### Will I lose my data if I cancel Pro?
No. Cancelling Pro will not delete any of your applications. Pro only unlocks additional capacity and features beyond the free tier.

### How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com), sign in with your Apple ID, and request a refund for Jaavo. If you need help, email us at the address above.

### The share extension isn't pulling job details from a posting.
Jaavo's auto-fill works best with LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and Handshake. Some sites block automated reading — in that case you can still paste the URL and fill in the details manually. Email us the URL if you'd like us to add better support for that site.
